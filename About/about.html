About F&R Energy-2024

United Scientific Group a non-profit organization takes the honor to invite you to the Fourth International Conference on Fossil & Renewable Energy which will be held on February 17-19, 2020 at Houston, TX. F&R Energy-2020 hosts important gathering and foster connections that will spur the innovations in Energy fields.

F&R Energy-2020 formerly titled as Gas, Oil & Petroleum Engineering (GOPE) and now reinstated with a new dialogue on Energy Ideas. It is a forum where business and academia intersect to spark development and showcase latest technological developments in Global Energy Issues.

It is a three-day event which features energy experts, academicians, business executives and engineers to showcase recent trends, strategies and challenges of energy systems. It creates platform to focus on advancing new energy paradigms for energy systems and global energy issues. F&R Energy-2020 program is an assemblage of inspirational keynote addresses, top-notch educational sessions, interactive panels and networking events.

We believe F&R Energy-2020 is an opportunity to network with peers and take real ideas to implement at your respective organizations.

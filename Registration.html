Registration Includes:
  • Access to all Scientific Sessions, Poster sessions, Exhibitions
  • Handbook & Conference Materials
  • Breakfast, Lunch, tea / coffee breaks during the conference days
  • Certificate Accreditation from the Organizing Committee
  • Access to the attendees email list (post conference)
  • WiFi in meeting rooms
Poster Presentation Includes:
  • Includes all mentioned registration features
  • Poster cards with number will be placed on the poster hall before one hour of the session start
  • Each author will be provided with a 1 meter x 1 meter poster presenting area and mounting pins / stick-tape. Participants are responsible for mounting their posters during the presentation and in removing them as soon as the session ends.
  • Best Poster awards will be announced during the closing ceremony of the conference.
Delegate Registration Includes:
  • Access to all Scientific Sessions, Poster sessions, Exhibitions
  • Breakfast, Lunch, tea / coffee breaks during the conference days
  • Certificate Accreditation from the Organizing Committee
  • Delegates are not allowed to present their papers in Oral or Poster sessions.
Cancellation Policy:
  • Cancellations should be made in writing.
  • Before 45 days of the conference will receive a full refund, less a $100 towards administrative fee.
  • The registrations with discounted prices are transferable but not refundable at any point of time.
  • Refunds will be made in the second week after the completion of the conference.
